Why Pinterest?

Pinterest is a very important platform, not only because 98% of its users are looking for a purchase inspiration but also users are 3x more likely to click over to brands website than any other social media platform.
At the same time it perfectly reflects our target audience (60% of Pinterest users are women and it's frequently used by millenials).


(Data source)

Type of content

Content we produce for Pinterest is to be two kinds, informative and infographical.
The first to accommodate the users that are looking for new sustainable products and the latter to satisfy the interest of our target group as well as getting the Nuuii's name out there to a person who necessarily isn't searching for cleaning products but for a fun infographic. This way our brand becomes more well-known within our target group.

Approach

Styling of the content can be more creatively free, using various color palettes and styles. However there always should be a drawn connection to the Nuuii brand and that includes the logo.
